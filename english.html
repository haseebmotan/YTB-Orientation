Hello friends, welcome to Ankara. This group is created to organize orientation for new students coming to Ankara within the scope of Türkiye Scholarships. Follow the information in the group and do not leave the group. Monday (25.09.2023) we will pick you up from your dormitories and take you to TÖMER, where we will help you with TÖMER/University registration and you'll also be able to visit your campus. We added most of the students on our list to this group, but we were unable to find a small number of students on WhatsApp. Therefore, we will be glad if you share this information with your friends living in the same dormitory. Shuttle service will be in front of your dormitories at 7:45 so make your preparations accordingly. It may be 5-10 minutes late from the scheduled time in the morning due to the traffic density. However, it is important that you wait.


Hello guys In order to complete the faculty registrations for undergraduate students, you must obtain an equivalence certificate from the Ministry of National Education (Milli eğitim bakanlığı). To get the equivalence certificate, you need to make the first online appointment. You can apply from the link https://edenklik.meb.gov.tr. YTB ​​will make your bank account and health insurance. Keep track of your e-mails and TBBS systems. YTB ​​has sent your YU (Foreign National) numbers to you already.
